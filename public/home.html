Quick tips

📅
Book early for peak seasons

July–October is migration season in the Mara — lodges fill up fast.

💉
Yellow fever certificate

Required for entry into many East African countries. Sort this out well ahead of travel.

💵
Budget in USD or KES

Most safari lodges price in USD. Local transport and food is cheaper in local currency.
